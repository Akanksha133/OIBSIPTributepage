The Legendary Queen of Music

Lata Mangeshkar

Lata Mangeshkar:It was Bollywood leading ladies. She lent her voice to over 5,000 songs in over a thousand Hindi and 36 regional films. She had unprecedented impact on Indian film music.

Biography

Lata was born on September 28, 1929, to classical singer and theatre artist Pandit Deenanath Mangeshkar and Shevanti in Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Her father started teaching her music at an early age.

By the time she was five, Lata was seen participating as an actress in plays written by her father.
